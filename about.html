Thank you for your interest in my work. Currently the only product on this site is Grid Framework, the editor and scripting extension for the Unity3D game engine that adds grids, but I want to increase my range of products in the future. To learn more about Grid Framework please visit its entry on this site.

If you are looking for support, have questions or just want to share your opinion the best way is to visit the official Grid Framework thread over at the Unity forums. I get an instant notification, so I won't miss your post, and the syntax highlighting is better for answering questions than other forms of contact. If you are looking for the latest news you can visit my blog or my YouTube channel.
